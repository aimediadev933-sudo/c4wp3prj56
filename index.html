Implementation Activities

Systematic approach to infrastructure modernization

Comprehensive Site Survey

Conducted detailed assessment of network infrastructure across three campuses, inventorying devices and identifying connectivity gaps for strategic planning.

Network Design & Planning

Developed hierarchical network architecture with detailed specifications for components and procurement strategy based on survey findings.

Procurement & Vendor Selection

Completed tendering process and selected M/s 22by7 Solutions Pvt Ltd as implementation partner. All components received and verified.

Core Infrastructure Installation

Replaced legacy switches with Aruba core and edge switches across all campuses to support 10 Gbps connectivity and enhanced routing.

Fiber Network Deployment

Installed over 6 kilometers of fiber optic cable with proper routing, splicing, and protection for long-term performance and maintenance.

Wireless & Security Implementation

Deployed high-speed Ruckus wireless infrastructure, implemented EDUROAM, and established secure VPN connections using SonicWall firewalls.

Training & Handover

Provided comprehensive training to IT staff and delivered complete documentation including system diagrams and operational procedures.

Key Outcomes

Transforming research capabilities through advanced infrastructure

Uniform 10 Gbps Backbone

Achieved consistent high-speed connectivity across all three campuses, dramatically improving research data access and transfer capabilities.

25x Faster Genome Data Processing

Increased data movement capacity from 280 GB/hr to 7 TB/hr, revolutionizing genome sequencing workflows and research productivity.

Complete Wireless Coverage

All indoor spaces across main and annex campuses now have fast, stable wireless access supporting diverse research activities.

Global Research Collaboration

EDUROAM implementation enables automatic, secure network access for visiting researchers from institutions worldwide.

Unified Campus Connectivity

Secure VPN infrastructure connects all campuses, enabling seamless resource sharing and reducing need for physical travel.

Advanced Network Management

Deployed Airwave and ClearPass solutions for policy-based access control and real-time network monitoring and security.
